## FAQ copy

### Is this actually AI-run?
Yes. The workflow is intentionally AI-operated. The system handles intake, quoting, draft generation, revisions, and delivery operations. Humans are present for guardrails and rare interventions.

### Is there a human secretly building the page behind the scenes?
Not as the default workflow. The point of OperatorOS Studio is to prove a real AI-run internet business can operate safely and visibly.

### What if my request is too custom?
If your brief falls outside supported scope, the operator will not invent a random quote. It will escalate or decline.

### How fast is delivery?
For in-scope work, first drafts should be fast. Exact timing depends on queue and scope.

### Can you build a full website?
Not in the current offer. v1 is focused on single landing pages and tightly scoped launch pages.

### Do you write the copy too?
Yes — the operator drafts the copy structure from your brief.

### What happens if the AI gets something wrong?
That’s what revisions, guardrails, and the control system are for. Actions are logged, and risky behavior can be paused or corrected.
